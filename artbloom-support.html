FAQ

How do I save a draft?
On the Canvas tab, tap Save Draft in the top bar. Drafts appear under Studio → Drafts.

How do I import a photo?
In the canvas tool panel, tap the photo icon and choose an image from your library.

Where is my artwork stored?
Artworks, drafts, and settings are saved locally on your device. We do not require an account.

How do I clear cached images?
Go to Studio → Settings → Storage → Clear Cache.

How do I change language or dark mode?
Open Studio → Settings → Preferences.

Report a Problem
