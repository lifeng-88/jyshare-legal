DiaryL Privacy Policy

DiaryL 隐私政策

Effective date: July 12, 2026
Applies to: DiaryL (Bundle ID: com.lensai.diary)

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 12 日
适用应用：DiaryL（Bundle ID：com.lensai.diary）

1. Overview

1. 概述

Welcome to DiaryL (the “App”). We value your privacy. This Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

欢迎使用 DiaryL（以下简称「本应用」）。我们重视您的隐私。本政策说明本应用如何收集、使用、存储与保护您的信息。使用本应用即表示您已阅读并理解本政策。

DiaryL is a journaling tool focused on on-device records. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not upload your diary content to our servers.

DiaryL 是一款以本地记录为核心的日志工具。我们不会要求您注册账号，也不会将您的日记内容上传至我们的服务器。

2. Information We Process

2. 我们处理的信息

To provide App features, DiaryL may process the following information locally on your device:

为实现产品功能，本应用可能在您的设备本地处理以下信息：

  • Journal content: titles, notes, dates, moods, tags, entry types (daily / anniversary), and other items you create, edit, or import.
  • 日志内容：标题、备注、日期、心情、主题标签、日志类型（日常 / 纪念日）等，由您主动创建、编辑或导入。
  • Preferences: language, appearance mode, reminder settings and times, background-related settings, and similar choices.
  • 偏好设置：语言、外观模式、提醒开关与时间、背景图相关设置等。
  • Background images: if you choose a custom photo or a built-in style image, it is stored only in the App sandbox on your device.
  • 背景图片：若您从相册选择自定义背景，或使用内置风格图，图片仅保存在本机应用沙盒中。
  • Notification-related data: if you enable reminders, the App schedules local notifications (for example, daily writing reminders or anniversary reminders).
  • 通知相关数据：若您开启提醒，本应用会在本地安排通知（例如每日写作提醒、纪念日提醒）。
  • Device debug identifier (dev_id): a random identifier generated for limited debugging scenarios, stored in the device Keychain, and not used for advertising tracking.
  • 设备调试标识（dev_id）：在特定调试场景下生成的随机标识，保存在本机 Keychain，不用于广告追踪。
  • Backup files: if you export or import a JSON backup, the related files are saved to or read from locations you choose.
  • 备份文件：若您导出或导入 JSON 备份，相关文件由您自行选择保存或读取的位置。

3. How Information Is Stored

3. 信息如何存储

  • Journal data is stored on your device using the system local database (SwiftData).
  • 日志数据通过系统本地数据库（SwiftData）保存在您的设备上。
  • Settings are stored in on-device preferences storage.
  • 设置项保存在本机偏好存储中。
  • dev_id is stored in the device Keychain.
  • dev_id 保存在本机 Keychain。
  • The App does not operate a cloud account system and, by default, does not sync diary content to developer servers.
  • 本应用不运营云端账号体系，默认不会将日记内容同步到开发者服务器。

4. System Permissions

4. 系统权限说明

  • Notifications: used only when you enable reminders, to deliver local alerts.
  • 通知权限：仅在您开启提醒功能时使用，用于发送本地提醒。
  • Photo access: used only when you select a custom background image; the App does not scan your photo library without your action.
  • 照片访问权限：仅在您选择自定义背景图时使用，用于读取您指定的图片；本应用不会在未经您操作的情况下扫描相册。

You can turn off these permissions in system settings at any time. Related features may become unavailable, but other local journaling features remain available.

您可随时在系统设置中关闭相关权限；关闭后对应功能可能不可用，但不影响其他本地记录功能。

5. How We Use Information

5. 信息的使用目的

We use the information above only to:

我们仅将上述信息用于：

  • provide core features such as journaling, calendar views, search and filters, statistics, and backups;
  • 提供日志记录、日程展示、筛选搜索、统计与备份等核心功能；
  • display language, appearance, and backgrounds according to your preferences;
  • 按您的偏好展示语言、外观与背景；
  • send local reminders when you enable them;
  • 在您开启时发送本地提醒；
  • troubleshoot issues or perform necessary product debugging (for example, copying a dev_id).
  • 排查问题或进行必要的产品调试（如复制 dev_id）。

We do not use your diary content for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

我们不会将您的日记内容用于广告投放，也不会出售您的个人信息。

6. Sharing and Third Parties

6. 信息共享与第三方

Except where required by law, or necessary to protect users and public safety, we do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your diary content with third parties.

除非法律法规要求，或为保护用户与公众安全所必需，我们不会向第三方出售、出租或以其他方式共享您的日记内容。

The App may rely on Apple system capabilities (such as notifications, photo pickers, and local storage). Those capabilities are governed by Apple’s related privacy policies and system mechanisms.

本应用可能依赖苹果公司提供的系统能力（如通知、照片选择器、本地存储）。这些能力受苹果相关隐私政策与系统机制约束。

7. Data Security

7. 数据安全

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect local data, including sandbox isolation and Keychain storage for sensitive identifiers. However, no electronic storage method can guarantee absolute security. Please keep your device secure and export backups regularly if needed.

我们采取合理的技术与管理措施保护本地数据，包括应用沙盒隔离、Keychain 存储敏感标识等。但请理解，任何电子存储方式都无法保证绝对安全。建议您妥善保管设备，并定期自行导出备份。

8. Retention and Deletion

8. 数据保留与删除

  • You may edit or delete journal entries in the App at any time.
  • 您可随时在应用内编辑或删除日志记录。
  • Uninstalling the App usually removes data in the App sandbox. Keychain identifiers may remain on the device in some cases until overwritten or cleared by the system.
  • 卸载应用通常会删除应用沙盒内数据；Keychain 中的标识在某些情况下可能仍保留于设备，直至被覆盖或系统清理。
  • Exported backup files are managed and deleted by you.
  • 您导出的备份文件由您自行管理与删除。

9. Children’s Privacy

9. 未成年人保护

The App is intended for general users. If you are a minor, please use it under a guardian’s guidance and avoid including unnecessary sensitive personal information in your journals.

本应用面向一般用户。若您是未成年人，请在监护人指导下使用，并避免在日志中填写不必要的敏感个人信息。

10. Policy Updates

10. 政策更新

We may update this Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page and take effect based on the stated effective date. If changes materially affect your rights, we will provide reasonable notice.

我们可能适时更新本政策。更新后的版本将在本页面发布，并以「生效日期」为准。若更新内容对您的权利有重大影响，我们将通过合理方式提示您。

11. 联系我们

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or personal data processing, please use in-app feedback channels or contact the DiaryL developer using the contact methods published on the App Store listing.

如对本隐私政策或个人数据处理有任何疑问，请通过应用内反馈渠道，或向 DiaryL 开发者发送邮件联系（请以应用商店页面公布的联系方式为准）。

This Policy applies only to DiaryL’s on-device client features. If we later add cloud sync, account sign-in, or other online services, we will update this Policy and obtain your consent again where required.
本政策仅适用于 DiaryL 客户端本地功能。若未来增加云同步、账号登录或其他联网服务，我们将同步更新本政策并再次征得您的同意（如适用）。
